WHAT IS FINAL FANTACY XVI ?

Final Fantasy XVI is the sixteenth main installment in the Final Fantasy series, developed and published by Square Enix. Released in June 2023, it marks a departure from the series' traditional turn-based combat, opting instead for a real-time action RPG system. The game is set in the world of Valisthea, a land dominated by powerful Mothercrystals and embroiled in a conflict between nations. Players control Clive Rosfield, a protector sworn to shield his younger brother Joshua, who is the Dominant of Phoenix, a powerful Eikon. The story delves into themes of fate, revenge, and the cyclical nature of war, featuring a mature and darker tone than previous entries in the franchise. While maintaining core Final Fantasy elements like summons (Eikons), the game's narrative and gameplay offer a fresh experience for longtime fans and newcomers alike.
