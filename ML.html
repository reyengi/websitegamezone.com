What is Mobile Legends?

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) mobile game developed by Moonton. It's a 5v5 team battle game where players control a character ("hero") with unique abilities and work together to destroy the opposing team's base. The gameplay involves strategic team composition, map awareness, and skillful execution of hero abilities. The game is known for its fast-paced action and competitive nature, with a large global player base. It's free-to-play, though in-app purchases are available for cosmetic items and hero unlocks.
