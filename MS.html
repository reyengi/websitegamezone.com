WHAT IS MARVEL SNAP ?

Marvel Snap is a fast-paced, collectible card game developed by Second Dinner and published by Nuverse. It features characters and elements from the Marvel universe, where players build decks of cards and strategically deploy them across three different locations in quick, six-turn matches. The game emphasizes quick decision-making and strategic card placement, as players aim to control locations and outmaneuver their opponent. Its collectible card game (CCG) mechanics are combined with a simplified, easy-to-learn approach to the genre, making it accessible to both seasoned CCG players and newcomers. Marvel Snap is known for its short matches, its engaging gameplay loop, and its use of popular Marvel characters, which makes it appealing to both fans of the Marvel universe and fans of collectible card games.
