WHAT IS HONOR OF KINGS ?

Honor of Kings (王者荣耀，Wáng zhě róng yào) is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) mobile game developed and published by Tencent Games. The gameplay involves two teams of five players controlling heroes with unique abilities, battling to destroy the opposing team's base. The game features a large roster of heroes, each with distinct strengths and weaknesses, prompting strategic team composition and dynamic gameplay.
