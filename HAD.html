WHAT IS HADES II ?

Hades II is an upcoming action role-playing game (ARPG) developed by Supergiant Games, the creators of the critically acclaimed Hades. It's a sequel that builds upon the gameplay and storytelling of the original, maintaining its signature blend of fast-paced combat, rogue-like elements, and rich narrative. While details are still emerging, Hades II is expected to feature a new protagonist, Melinoë, the Princess of the Underworld, and a new storyline focused on her efforts to stop the Titans from escaping their prison. Players will again navigate the ever-changing Underworld, encountering a diverse cast of characters, engaging in challenging battles, and forging relationships through dialogue choices that influence the narrative. The game will likely retain the core gameplay loop of the first game, which involved repeated runs through procedurally generated levels, collecting powerful weapons and abilities, and improving the protagonist's skills to overcome increasingly difficult challenges.
