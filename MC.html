WHAT IS MINECRAFT ?

Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang Studios. It's known for its blocky, pixelated graphics and its emphasis on creative freedom and exploration. Players can mine for resources, build structures, craft items, and explore procedurally generated worlds, either alone or with others in multiplayer mode. Minecraft offers various game modes, including Survival mode (where players must gather resources to survive), Creative mode (where players have unlimited resources and can build freely), and Adventure mode (where players explore pre-designed levels).
