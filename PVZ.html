WHAT IS PVZ: GARDEN WARFARE ?

Plants vs. Zombies: Garden Warfare (often abbreviated as PVZ GW or GW1) is a 2014 third-person shooter video game developed by PopCap Games and published by Electronic Arts. It's a spin-off from the popular tower defense game, Plants vs. Zombies, but shifts the gameplay to a team-based, multiplayer experience. Players choose to control either plants or zombies, each with unique character classes possessing distinct abilities and play styles. The game features a variety of competitive and cooperative modes, where players battle across a range of colorful maps. While retaining the lighthearted humor and distinctive art style of the original Plants vs. Zombies, Garden Warfare introduces a more action-oriented and strategic gameplay loop. The game was successful enough to spawn a sequel, Plants vs. Zombies: Garden Warfare 2.
