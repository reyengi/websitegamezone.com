WHAT IS RACING MASTER ?

Racing Master is a mobile racing game developed by NetEase Games that features realistic racing simulation and a wide variety of licensed cars. The game emphasizes authentic driving physics and car handling, aiming to provide a more realistic racing experience compared to many arcade-style mobile racing games. Players can choose from a selection of licensed vehicles from various manufacturers, each with its own unique handling characteristics and performance stats. The game offers different game modes, including career mode, where players progress through various races and championships, and multiplayer modes, allowing them to compete against other players online. Racing Master often highlights its detailed car models and tracks, aiming for visual fidelity and a sense of immersion. The game is free-to-play but offers in-app purchases for cosmetic items and upgrades.
