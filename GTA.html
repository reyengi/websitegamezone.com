WHAT IS GTA V ?

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is a critically acclaimed action-adventure game featuring three playable protagonists navigating the criminal underworld of Los Santos. The expansive open world offers a compelling single-player story filled with heists and missions, alongside the ever-evolving online world of GTA Online, where players can create their own criminal empires. Known for its immersive world, detailed characters, and controversial content, GTA V remains a cultural phenomenon and one of the best-selling video games ever.
