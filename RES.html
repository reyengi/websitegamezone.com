WHAT IS RESIDENT EVIL VILLAGE ?

Resident Evil Village, also known as Resident Evil 8: Village, is a 2021 first-person survival horror game developed and published by Capcom. It's a direct sequel to Resident Evil 7: Biohazard and continues the story of Ethan Winters. Village features a blend of first-person shooting, puzzle-solving, and resource management, hallmarks of the Resident Evil franchise. The game takes place in a mysterious, snow-covered village inhabited by various grotesque and terrifying creatures, each with their own unique characteristics and motivations. Players must navigate this hostile environment, scavenging for resources, solving puzzles, and utilizing a variety of weapons and tools to survive. The game's setting combines elements of classic horror with modern action, and features a compelling storyline exploring themes of family, survival, and the consequences of past actions. Village received critical acclaim for its atmosphere, visuals, and gameplay, solidifying its place as a significant entry in the Resident Evil franchise.
