WHAT IS PUBG MOBILE ?

PUBG Mobile is a massively popular free-to-play battle royale game available on Android and iOS devices. Developed by Lightspeed & Quantum Studio and PUBG Studios, and published globally by various companies, it brings the intense survival experience of PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds to mobile platforms. Up to 100 players parachute onto a large map, searching for weapons and equipment to eliminate opponents and become the last survivor. Players can compete solo, in duos, or in squads of up to four, collaborating to achieve victory. The game features a variety of maps, game modes, and regular updates introducing new content, weapons, and features, ensuring a consistently engaging experience for millions of players worldwide. Its accessibility and engaging gameplay have made it a global phenomenon.
