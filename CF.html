WHAT IS CROSSFIRE ?

Crossfire is a free-to-play first-person shooter (FPS) game developed by SmileGate. It's incredibly popular in many parts of Asia and has gained a global following. The gameplay typically involves two teams, usually "Black List" and "Global Risk," competing in various game modes such as Team Deathmatch, Demolition, and Search & Destroy. The game features a large arsenal of weapons, allowing players to customize their loadouts and playstyles. Crossfire is known for its fast-paced action, its competitive scene, and its accessibility as a free-to-play title.
